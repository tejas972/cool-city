🌿 Urban Tree Canopy

Urban tree canopies provide shade, reduce heat, and promote cleaner air and a better quality of life.

🍃 Benefits

  • Reduces surface and air temperature through shading
  • Enhances air quality by filtering pollutants
  • Sequesters carbon and improves biodiversity

🌳 Species Selection

Select native, drought-resistant trees with broad canopies. Popular choices include Neem, Gulmohar, and Banyan in Indian urban zones.

🛠️ Strategies

  • Street tree planting and care programs
  • Green corridors and parks
  • Community planting drives and stewardship
