🌱 Green Roofs

Explore how green roofs help cities stay cool while enhancing biodiversity and livability. Below are key aspects presented in elegant cards.

🔍 Benefits

  • Reduces ambient temperature by up to 3°C
  • Improves insulation and reduces energy consumption
  • Manages stormwater runoff effectively
  • Increases biodiversity and supports pollinators
  • Visually appealing and increases property value

🛠️ Implementation

  • Extensive: Lightweight, low-maintenance, ideal for homes.
  • Intensive: Supports deep soil, trees, and recreation zones.
  • Essential layers: waterproofing, drainage, filter, substrate, vegetation.

🌍 Global Examples

Cities like Toronto, Chicago, and Singapore lead in adopting green roofs. Singapore's "Gardens by the Bay" is a prime example of integrating green roofs into urban planning.

📊 Quick Facts

  • Green roofs can lower indoor temps by 5–10°C
  • Can last 30–50 years with proper maintenance
  • Often eligible for green building incentives
