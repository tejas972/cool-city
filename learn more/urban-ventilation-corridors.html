🌬️ Urban Ventilation Corridors

Ventilation corridors help circulate cool air through dense urban spaces, reducing heat accumulation and improving air quality.

🌬️ Principles

  • Understand urban wind patterns
  • Avoid blocking airflows with dense construction
  • Maximize north-south street orientation where possible

🏙️ Implementation

  • Design green open spaces along wind corridors
  • Minimize obstruction through zoning regulations
  • Encourage aerodynamic building forms

🌡️ Benefits

  • Reduces trapped heat in urban pockets
  • Improves pedestrian comfort and air quality
  • Contributes to passive cooling of buildings
