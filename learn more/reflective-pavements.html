☀️ Reflective Pavements

Reflective pavements help reduce the urban heat island effect by using materials that reflect rather than absorb heat.

💡 Types of Pavements

  • High-albedo asphalt and concrete
  • Cool coatings applied on existing surfaces
  • Permeable pavements that allow water infiltration

🌡️ Benefits

  • Lowers surface temperature by up to 20°C
  • Improves comfort for pedestrians and cyclists
  • Helps reduce energy use in surrounding buildings

🛠️ Considerations

  • Durability and long-term performance
  • Cost-effectiveness and maintenance frequency
  • Potential glare in some applications

🌍 Real-World Applications

Los Angeles has experimented with reflective coatings on streets, showing temperature drops of 10°F. Similar strategies are used in Phoenix and Tokyo.
